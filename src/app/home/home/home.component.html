Welcome to your online banking solution!

I structured the app the way I typically structure an Angular app. On landing, you have the home module where you can click on the sub-modules (only loans), but on a real app, you would see a credit card module, online brokerage, etc. On the sub-module of the loans, you will be able to see the different types of loans and tools to help you pick the loans (only a mortgage calculator right now).
